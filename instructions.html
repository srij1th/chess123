How to Play Chess

Goal: Checkmate the opponent king.

  • White moves first.
  • Each player moves one piece per turn.
  • Special rules included: castling, en passant, promotion.
  • Draw can happen by stalemate.

Game Controls

  • Click a piece to select it.
  • Highlighted squares are valid moves.
  • Click a highlighted square to move.
